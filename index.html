Main Features
Manage & Host Apps and News Manage your applications and messages with a beautiful and intuitive UI that will be familiar to both beginners and anyone who has ever distributed an application.
Feedback & Review System Collect feedback from your users and let them leave reviews and rate your apps.
(Requires modified version of SideStore)
Localized Texts Provide localized app titles and subtitles, as well as app and version descriptions to your users.
Webhooks Use Webhooks to upload new builds, update version information and publish new releases, all from your CI pipeline (e.g. GitHub Actions, fastlane)!
User & Organization Management Manage your applications across multiple organizations and invite more users to collaborate. Apply roles to limit the permissions of each user for easy collaboration.
Import Your Source You already have made a source for your apps? That's great! You can easily import your source into SideStore Connect and enjoy the benefits of a unified platform and a centralized source.
Deploy to all stores SideStore Connect is a community-driven platform that allows you to deploy your apps to all AltSource v1 and v2 compatible sideloading stores (e.g. AltStore, SideStore, and TrollStore) at once.
With SideStore Connect being the community app source that is pre-configured in SideStore, users can install your apps with a single tap. No need to manually add your source.
All apps provided by SideStore Connect are reviewed by the community admins to ensure that only safe apps are distributed.
Made in the EU SideStore Connect is developed in and hosted Germany and respects the user's privacy and data minimalism.
We only collect and store the data that is absolutely required to provide the service and nothing more.
